---
title: "Lia of the Jungle"
pubDate: 2026-07-08
description: "A tale of nature calling more loudly than expected!"
---

On my first expedition to the Amazon forest, as an inexperienced, recently graduated professional, I got lost for an hour in the middle of the jungle after deciding to leave the sampling site to go to the toilet. Yes, Mother Nature called, and I made the terrible decision of distancing myself from the sampling site, which had a massive blue tent that I thought would be easily detectable. I was wrong. I didn't go far, but it was enough to keep the blue tent from being visible! I decided to wait and not move for the next hour to hear anyone from the team approaching the site. All sorts of thoughts came through: 'It's better not to shout to save energy; is there any source of water around?; I wonder if I'll make it until the next day; it will be so embarrassing if they find me'. I was eventually found! And the team wanted me to be attached to a cord so I wouldn't get lost again. I can laugh about it, and that never-ending hour in the jungle taught me a lot. - Lia
