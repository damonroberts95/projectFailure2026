Al and Hu are locked in a lab in 1928, racing against expiring funding to perfect Hu's
sound-and-oxygen-blocking concentration device, "The Isolator." Four experiments, four
disasters — a whistling kettle, malfunctioning lights, egg salad fumes, and one very
persistent fly — later, Al's sandwich falls in the wrong petri dish.

That sandwich accidentally discovers penicillin.
